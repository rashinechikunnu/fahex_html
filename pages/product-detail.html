Kasthuri Manjal Powder

Weight: 50 g
₹ 120
Description

Fahex Kasthuri Manjal Powder is carefully sourced from pure Wild Turmeric (Curcuma aromatica) and ground using traditional stone methods to retain its full natural potency. Unlike common turmeric, Kasthuri Manjal has a delightful fragrance and is completely safe for facial use.

Benefits:

  • Reduces acne, pimples, and skin blemishes
  • Lightens tan, dark spots, and pigmentation
  • Improves natural skin glow and radiance
  • Anti-bacterial and anti-inflammatory properties
  • Safe for all skin types including sensitive skin
  • No chemicals or artificial additives

How to Use: Mix a small amount with water, rose water, or milk to form a paste. Apply on face and neck, leave for 10-15 minutes, then rinse with water.
