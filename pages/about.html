Our Story

About Us

Fahex Nature Glow is a traditional herbal skincare brand rooted in the rich heritage of Kasthuri Manjal (Wild Turmeric). Founded with the mission to bring pure and natural skincare solutions, we carefully harvest and prepare Kasthuri Manjal using age-old techniques that preserve its natural goodness.

Our products are free from chemicals, artificial fragrances, and harmful preservatives. We believe that nature has everything your skin needs — and Kasthuri Manjal is one of nature's most potent gifts for radiant, healthy skin.

Located in Malappuram, Kerala, we serve customers across India and beyond, delivering the authentic fragrance and benefit of wild turmeric directly to your doorstep.

About Kasthuri Manjal

Kasthuri Manjal, also known as Wild Turmeric, is a natural herbal ingredient used in beauty and skincare for centuries. Known for its refreshing fragrance and powerful skin benefits.
